An llms.txt file lives at yoursite.com/llms.txt and gives AI crawlers a machine-readable summary of your most important facts: who you are, what you do, your key services, correct contact details. Without one, AI models infer facts about your business from your general page content — which leads to errors, omissions, and misrepresentation. It functions like robots.txt but is designed for language models rather than search crawlers. Legible generates yours automatically, and your Legibility Score drops significantly without one.
