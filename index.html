What is an llms.txt file?

An llms.txt file is a short text document that lives at yoursite.com/llms.txt. It tells ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and other AI assistants exactly who you are, what you do, and what you want them to know about your business — in a format they can read reliably. Think of it as a business card written specifically for AI. Without one, AI models piece together information from your general web pages and frequently get things wrong. Legible generates yours automatically. Here is ours as a live example:
